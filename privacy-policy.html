Privacy Policy — Aura Block

Last updated: May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Aura Block (“the app”, “we”, “our”) handles information when you use our mobile game on Android (and other platforms where published). The app is published by the developer listed on Google Play.

1. Summary

  • We do not require an account or collect your name, email, or phone number in the app.
  • Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • The app shows ads (Google AdMob) and offers in-app purchases through Google Play.
  • Third-party services may collect data according to their own policies (see below).

2. Information stored on your device

The app uses local storage (Android SharedPreferences) to save data needed to play, for example: high scores, gold balance, theme and cosmetic choices, sound and vibration settings, daily quest progress, streaks, and similar game state. This data stays on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the app.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Aura Block displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, including banner ads on the home screen and rewarded video ads (for example to earn in-game gold or continue playing). Google may collect and use information such as advertising identifiers, device information, and interaction with ads to serve and measure ads, in line with Google’s Privacy Policy and AdMob & Ads policies.

You can limit ad personalization in your device settings (Google account / Ads settings on Android). If you purchase “Remove ads” through the app, ad units are disabled for your install after purchase is verified.

4. In-app purchases (Google Play)

Purchases (for example remove ads, theme unlocks, booster packs) are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive your full payment card details. Google handles payment according to Google’s Privacy Policy. Purchase status may be stored locally on your device to unlock content.

5. Network access

The app uses the internet to load ads, complete purchases, and download optional assets (for example fonts). No account registration is required to play.

6. Vibration

If enabled in settings, the app may trigger short device vibration for feedback. You can turn vibration off in the app settings.

7. Children

Aura Block is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. Data retention and deletion

Local game data remains on your device until you clear storage for Aura Block or uninstall the app. Data held by Google (ads, Play purchases) is governed by Google’s policies and your Google account settings.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
